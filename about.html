About Me
Hi, I'm Rosie! Welcome to my shop.

When I was little, my dream was to paint. It's always been my favourite thing to do, and in between training as a counsellor, I'm still doing it.

After hearing people tell me they'd like to have my paintings in their homes, I decided to start a little store and sell some of them. I'm very picky, so I had to be patient whilst I found a printing process I could be proud of, and now that I do, I'm more than happy to present them to you.

I love the delicate, elegant and pretty things in life, and I love to include them in the prints I sell. Because I hand draw each piece, I can make additions to make it personal - if you love roses, I can add some! Just message me for any request, I'm happy to work with each person to create something lovely.

I hope you love my little shop as much as I do!
